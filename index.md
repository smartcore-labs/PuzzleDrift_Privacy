# Privacy Policy for Puzzle Drift

**Effective Date: March 16, 2026**

At **Puzzle Drift**, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

---

### I. Information We Collect
We collect minimal data to provide a better gaming experience:
*   **Game Progress**: Level completion, best moves, and completion times are stored locally on your device and synced with **Firebase** leaderboard services.
*   **Device Information**: We use **AdMob** and **Firebase Analytics** to understand app performance and display relevant ads.

### II. Permissions We Use
To provide all gameplay features, we request the following permissions:
*   **INTERNET**: To sync your leaderboard scores and display ads.
*   **BILLING**: To process optional in-app purchases (e.g., Removing Ads).
*   **CAMERA**: Used only when you choose to "Create From Photo" to turn a real-world picture into a puzzle. No photos are uploaded to our servers.
*   **STORAGE**: Used to allow you to pick an image from your gallery for use as a puzzle.

### III. Data Protection
*   We do not sell your data to third parties.
*   Your photos used for custom puzzles are processed **entirely on your device** and are never sent to our servers.

### IV. Children's Privacy
Puzzle Drift is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
